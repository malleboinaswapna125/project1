St. Mary always preaches and practices the “student- first approach”. It is in- fact the core strength of St. Mary’s group of institutions is a unique concept that creates a strong bond between the management, parents and the students. It provides important inputs to the administrator for excellence in governance. Accessibility is not only the approach, it is a commitment by the management to provide 24/7 access to infrastructure and facilities for the well-being, improvement and career progress of the student.?

Transparency is about the interaction with both parents and students, making each stakeholder on the improvement and continue the development of students’ aptitude and attitude. Accountability is an assurance to comply with the rules and regulations, a promise to the parents to provide an overall development to their ward’s future.
